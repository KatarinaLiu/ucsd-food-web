I need food today

  • Triton Food Pantry — check today's hours & locations
  • UCSD Basic Needs Hub — official food support entry

Common hesitation: “I’m not sure I qualify.” Don’t self-reject—start with Pantry guidance first, then follow official eligibility steps.

I may run out this week

  • Triton Food Pantry — recurring access & schedule
  • UCSD Basic Needs Hub — overview of food resources

First move: Pick one option you can do within 24 hours (usually Pantry). Action beats perfect planning.

Food stress is long-term

  • CalFresh — eligibility & application support
  • Basic Needs Emergency Grant — short-term financial relief

Common hesitation: “I don’t know if I’m eligible.” Treat that as a question for the office, not a reason to stop.

FAQ

Will using food support affect my financial aid / record?
I don’t interpret policy here. Please follow official UCSD Basic Needs guidance and contact them directly if you’re unsure.

I feel awkward using these resources.
That feeling is common. Food access is a campus reality, and these services exist because students need them.

Policies and hours may change. Always confirm via official pages.

About

This page is part of Food Web, a student-led initiative informed by observations of how students navigate (or avoid) existing food resources, aiming to reduce confusion, stigma, and “perceived ineligibility” barriers.
